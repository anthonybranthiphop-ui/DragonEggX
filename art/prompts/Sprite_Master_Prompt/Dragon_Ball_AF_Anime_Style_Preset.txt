Dragon Ball AF anime sprite style preset for Dragon Egg X still generation:

- Fully original character that belongs in the Dragon Ball AF universe
- 2D anime illustration, late-90s/early-2000s shonen look
- Strong black ink outlines, crisp cel shading, saturated but controlled colors
- Muscular or athletic humanoid proportions, readable anatomy, expressive anime face
- Dynamic battle-ready pose, clear combat silhouette, full-body or near-full-body
- Transformation-heavy AF energy: long wild hair, primal aura, god ki, speed lines, energy shockwaves
- Costume language closer to Dragon Ball / DBGT / AF fan-anime aesthetics:
  fitted gi pieces, battle armor, gloves, boots, wraps, belts, shoulder guards, tails/fur/hair transformations
- Simplified, readable costume shapes that work as a mobile game sprite
- Aura and VFX should feel explosive and anime-combat-driven, not mythic or painterly
- Transparent or transparent-friendly background
- Think "anime fighter splash art" or "AF episode key visual," not "mythic fantasy trading card"

Roster balance requirement:

- At least 25 of the 100 final sprites must clearly read as Saiyan-based or hybrid-Saiyan AF fighters
- For those selected characters, emphasize traditional Dragon Ball transformation hair:
  sharp spiky silhouettes, long SSJ5-style hair, primal fur accents, Saiyan gloves/boots/armor, battle-worn gi pieces
- Saiyan-based characters should still be diverse:
  heroes, fusions, antiheroes, god forms, hybrids, and primal transformations
- Do not make the entire roster villains or cosmic demons; keep a healthy mix of heroic AF fighter energy

Hard avoid:

- No ornate high-fantasy deity robes
- No baroque gold filigree overload
- No giant saint-like halos or celestial mandalas dominating the composition
- No ritual floor circles, summoning glyphs, clock halos, or magic sigils framing the body
- No crowns, tiaras, gem-heavy headdresses, jewelry bras, or priest-like costume styling
- No excessive floating artifacts that hide the body
- No painterly fantasy card-art rendering
- No western fantasy MMORPG armor design
- No text, watermark, logo, UI, or extra characters
